FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started

How do I create an account?
Simply click the "Register" button and choose your role (Job Seeker or Employer). Fill in your details and you're ready to start your journey.
Is the platform free for job seekers?
Yes! Creating a profile, searching jobs, and applying are completely free for job seekers. We believe everyone should have access to great career opportunities.
How does the AI matching work?
Our AI analyzes your skills, experience, preferences, and career goals to match you with the most relevant positions. The more you use the platform, the smarter the recommendations become.
Can employers post international jobs?
Absolutely! Our platform supports job postings from anywhere in the world. You can specify location preferences and remote work options for maximum reach.
